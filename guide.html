10 AI Marketing Automations Every Business Should Use

This is the kind of soft-conversion asset that keeps pipeline moving even when a visitor is not ready to book immediately. Each automation below removes delay, manual follow-up, or wasted ad spend.

1. Instant lead routing

Push every form fill into the right rep, inbox, CRM stage, and notification channel in real time.

2. AI lead scoring

Score inbound leads using firmographic data, source quality, urgency, and buying signals before sales ever touches them.

3. Missed-call text back

Automatically text back missed callers so high-intent prospects do not drop off.

4. Proposal follow-up sequences

Trigger smart reminders after proposals are sent, with timing based on view activity and deal stage.

5. Review request automation

Ask happy clients for testimonials and platform reviews at the right time without manual chasing.

6. CRM enrichment

Fill missing company size, industry, location, and role data so segmentation improves automatically.

7. Ad lead cleanup

Normalize paid lead data from Meta, Google, and LinkedIn so attribution stays usable.

8. Re-engagement campaigns

Restart cold opportunities with AI-personalized email and SMS messaging tied to historical behavior.

9. Content repurposing workflows

Turn one long-form asset into LinkedIn posts, email snippets, landing page copy, and ad hooks.

10. Dashboard summaries

Send weekly AI-written performance summaries that highlight changes, risks, and recommended actions.
